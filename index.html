qhatushoop is a basic text editor in your browser. It is just like all of the other ones out there, except there's no ads. Anyways, there are a few things you can do.

- Align your text. This one's pretty important. Choose whether you want your text dead-center, or off to the left or right.
- Make your text bold or italicized. Yeah, 'nuff said.
- Make your text all caps. There's (for some reason) buttons to make your text all uppercase or lowercase.
- Make the first letters of all the words you typed capitalized. Okay, this one's actually semi-useful.
